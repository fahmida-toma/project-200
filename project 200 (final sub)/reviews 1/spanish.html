The shadow of the wind:

The book has such a different feel of the previously mentioned books and a great mystery with an amazing story arc. Settings are revisited along with characters. As you read the book you witness Daniel Sempere, the main character, grow from a 10-year-old boy with dreams of writing who lost his mother to a teen with a love interest, new relationships, and broken ones. All characters are amazing and well developed, and the detail is phenomenal.
